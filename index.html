I am an associate professor at the University of Seoul, working in Department of Statistics and leading Data Intelligence Lab. My research interests lie in various applications for data processing and management in language and vision domains. I am currently working on analyzing the behavior of retrieval-augmented generative models.
Updates:

  • January 2024: A journal paper accepted at Applied Soft Computing. This work proposes a novel method about unsupervised training for image-to-image translation.

  • May 2023: A journal paper accepted at IEEE Access. This work improves the capability of discriminator in GAN for complex scene generation by enhancing multi-scale representations.

  • March 2023: A journal paper accepted at IEEE Access. This work proposes a graph transformer encoder for improving the math word problem solvers by merging heterogeneous graphs.
