I am an associate professor at the University of Seoul, working in Department of Statistics and leading Data Intelligence Lab. My research interests lie in various applications for data processing and management in language and vision domains. I am currently working on training knowledge-grounded conversation models and exploring large language models for different modalities.
Updates:

  • May 2023: A journal paper accepted at IEEE Access. This work improves the capability of discriminator in GAN for complex scene generation by enhancing multi-scale representations.

  • March 2023: A journal paper accepted at IEEE Access. This work proposes a graph transformer encoder for improving the math word problem solvers by merging heterogeneous graphs.

  • December 2022: A journal paper accepted at IEEE Access. This work investigates knowledge-grounded conversation models to utilize a retriever to adopt knowledge sentences from external sources while training.
